Career

A timeline of my professional and academic milestones.

2022 - Present

Independent Research

2021 - 2022

Staff Data Scientist, Project Lead

Valo

Built libraries and pipelines for generating patient representations using high-dimensional, multimodal and longitudinal Electronic Health Records (EHR), identifying patient subgroups and powering downstream predictive models for clinical programs, as part of the drug discovery and development platform.

2018 - 2021

Lead Data Scientist, SupportLogic

Led the design and deployment of machine learning models powering a SaaS platform built on support case data, collaborating closely with customers and cross-functional teams to develop explainable and actionable ML systems. The work resulted in several granted patents and additional filings, and included models for case outcome prediction, complexity estimation, and hybrid recommender systems for engineer-case matching. I also helped build internal ML knowledge through documentation, presentations, and mentoring.

2017

Alpine Data Labs

Investigated bias in parking citation patterns in San Francisco through a collaboration with the nonprofit Tipping Point. Built data pipelines and models linking citation patterns to economic inequity, contributing to a report that informed policy changes in the SF Financial Justice Department.
